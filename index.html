Artist Statement

The cause and effect necessary to make art enthralls me. Ceramics though? This is what engages me. I imagine my fingers, activated, pressing fingertip pressure into the clay — and then knuckle notching a cylinder upward. I see myself as a potter, a prairie potter.

Curriculum Vitae

Shaping the sacred
from earth & fire

Jonathan Bridges is a full-time studio artist working in porcelain, bronze, iron, brass, and mixed media. His practice explores the tension between joy and brutality — creating ornate vessels and visceral sculptural forms that honor both the sacred and the profane. He holds an MFA from The University of Minnesota and a BFA from Alfred University, and has exhibited internationally from New York to Toyama, Japan.
