Featured Work

Tabernacle

2022 · Porcelain, brass, glass, gems & resin · 11" × 13" × 20"

Throughout the ages, man has held sacred the resting place of his departed loved ones. This piece offers a sacred space for the essence of one who has died and honors their unique life and the organic vessel of their soul.

As a maker, my urns must be worthy of what they contain. The decorative form and its variety of materials are secondary to its utilitarian function: a tabernacle of essence.

Artist Statement
The cause and effect necessary to make art enthralls me. Ceramics though? This is what engages me. I imagine my fingers, activated, pressing fingertip pressure into the clay — and then knuckle notching a cylinder upward. I see myself as a potter, a prairie potter.
— Jonathan Bridges
